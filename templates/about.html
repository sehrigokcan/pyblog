About Us

This project was accomplished by a group of programming trainees who fled to Netherlands as refugees and devoted to be software programmers with the help of volunteer programming course PyCoders that was founded by experienced IT experts with refugee backgrounds. After having a knowledge of Python programming for 5 months, we worked through Django web framework for a month and we finalized the course with a final project under the supervision of our mentors. Our final project is a blog that gives some practical information about living in Netherlands. You can find some articles, recent news and posts. You are free to create a membership on the site and comment all the articles and posts.
